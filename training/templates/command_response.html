Welcome to the Pragbot Semantics training portal. Read each prompt and try to get Junior, the robot, to do what the prompt says in a single sentence. Make sure to:
  • Use good punctuation: commas and periods where appropriate. Junior is picky about that.
  • Don't leave out articles (a/the) or prepositions.
  • Junior isn't very chatty. Don't call him by name, say hello, or ask him questions. Just tell him what you need him to do.
  • Talk to Junior like you might talk to someone who needs instructions from you. Don't try to use unnatural shorthand that sounds like something Tarzan might say.
The training phase after this one is simulation training where you will command an actual robot simulation. That's the fun part!
